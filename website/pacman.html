Welcome to Pacman

Created by: Kj Purcell

MacPan is a recreation of the classic game 'Pacman'! Maneuver your MacPan character using Up, Down, Left, and Right arrow keys through 3 unique maps, eating all the pellets and most importantly, escape the ghosts that are looking for revenge! The game features: 3 levels, 4 ghosts and an ingame hiscore tracker!
